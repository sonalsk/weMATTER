About Us



Why is it important?

It is often seen that women in both rural and urban areas are unaware of the basic rights and opportunities available for them from a very young age. This leads to manipulation, exploitation and harassment at home and even in the workplace.



How are we different?

#weMATTER is a centralised web application for women to come together and learn about their rights and available opportunities in different areas of life. We provide them with the basic resources and services needed to reach out for help in areas like healthcare, education, career and safety.
